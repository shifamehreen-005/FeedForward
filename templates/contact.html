Common Questions

Whether you're looking for food, volunteering, or just curious about how things work — this FAQ section covers the most common questions. From finding the nearest food bank to understanding our heatmap, we've made it easy for you to get the help you need.

How can I find the nearest food bank?

You can use the Explore page to search directly, or get help via the chatbot, voice bot, or even call our hotline for assistance.

How can I update my profile?

You can head to the Profile Page to make changes manually. If you’d prefer something quicker, just ask the chatbot or voice bot — they’ll walk you through it.

I’m a volunteer. How do I find my assigned distribution site?

You have a dedicated Volunteer Dashboard! You can also use the chatbot or voice bot to find your assigned site and get directions.

How can I donate food or funds?

Thank you for your generosity! You can visit the Donate section on the website to contribute food, funds, or time. Every little bit helps.

Who can I contact if I need help or have questions?

You can reach us through the Contact Us page, speak to a support agent via the chatbot, or call our hotline for urgent concerns.

Are there any transportation options or directions to food banks?

Yes! On the food bank's info page, you’ll find directions, transit options, and Google Maps links to help you get there easily.

What’s the heatmap I see on the site?

The heatmap shows areas with higher food demand and distribution activity. It's designed to help volunteers and staff plan better and understand where help is most needed.

Can I use the site in different languages?

Absolutely. Our chatbot and voice assistant support multiple languages, and you can switch the site’s language from the top-right menu.

Is the chatbot available 24/7?

Yep! The chatbot and voice bot are available 24/7 to help you find food, update your profile, or just answer your questions.
