Our Story

Founded in 1985, our community has grown from a small group of families into a network of over 850 homes—united by care, compassion, and a shared commitment to ending hunger. At the heart of this growth is our food bank, which has become a cornerstone of support for neighbors in need. Through food distribution, volunteer efforts, and community partnerships, we’ve built more than just a pantry—we’ve built a place where everyone is welcome, nourished, and empowered to thrive.
